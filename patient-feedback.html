Patient Feedback

Healius Limited and our subsidiary companies are committed to listening and responding to feedback from our patients. This enables us to further improve the quality of services we deliver. If you have any feedback you would like to share with us please complete the form below, this will be received via the Healius Patient Feedback email address. Please note that this process can take up to 20 working days depending on the subject matter of your feedback.

Alternatively you can write to us at:
